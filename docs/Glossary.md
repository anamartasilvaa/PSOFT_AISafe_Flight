# Glossary

This glossary defines the core domain terms for the AISafe system, establishing a common language between the development team and the client.

| English Term                  | Portuguese Term                | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|:------------------------------|:-------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Aircraft**                  | Aeronave                       | A physical instance of an aircraft in the fleet, identified by a unique registration number, possessing a specific seating capacity, features, and a current operational status.                                                                                   |
| **Aircraft Feature**          | Característica da Aeronave     | A specific attribute or capability of an individual aircraft (WiFi-enabled, special engine type), freely added and managed by the users.                                                                                                                           |
| **Aircraft Model**            | Modelo de Aeronave             | A technical specification shared by multiple aircraft, defining the manufacturer, model name, fuel capacity, maximum range, and cruising speed.                                                                                                                    |
| **Aircraft Status**           | Estado da Aeronave             | The current operational condition of an aircraft, such as Active, Inactive, or Under Maintenance.                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Airplane Certification**    | Certificação de Aeronave       | An official authorization indicating that a specific Aircraft Model is permitted to operate (depart or arrive) at a particular Airport.                                                                                                                            |
| **Airport**                   | Aeroporto                      | A location for departure or arrival of routes, uniquely identified by a 3-letter IATA code, containing runways and contact information.                                                                                                                            |
| **Airport Status**            | Estado do Aeroporto	           | The operational condition of an airport (Operational, Closed, Under Maintenance).                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Checklist Item**            | Item de Verificação            | A specific task or point of inspection that makes up the maintenance procedures defined in a Maintenance Template.                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Contact**                   | Contacto                       | Communication details associated with an airport, categorized by type (phone, email) and value.                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Coordinates**               | Coordenadas	                   | The geographical location of an airport, defined by its latitude and longitude.                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Facility**                  | Infraestrutura                 | A physical structure or service available at an airport, such as passenger terminals, gates, or specific operational services.                                                                                                                                     |
| **Flight Route**              | Rota de Voo                    | A planned connection between an origin airport and a destination airport, requiring a minimum range and capacity from any assigned aircraft.                                                                                                                       |
| **Flight Status**             | Estado do Voo                  | The lifecycle stages of a scheduled flight, including Scheduled, Delayed, In-Flight, Completed, and Canceled.                                                                                                                                                      |
| **IATA Code**                 | Código IATA                    | A standard 3-letter alphanumeric code designating an airport, used as its unique business identifier in the system.                                                                                                                                                |
| **Maintenance Part**          | Peça de Manutenção             | An inventory component managed by the maintenance supervisor, monitored via stock quantities to trigger alerts when levels fall below a minimum threshold.                                                                                                         |
| **Maintenance Record**        | Registo de Manutenção          | A record of a maintenance intervention performed on a specific aircraft. While undergoing maintenance, the aircraft cannot be assigned to any scheduled flights.                                                                                                   |
| **Maintenance Record Status** | Estado do Registo (manutenção) | The execution progress of a maintenance task: Scheduled, In Progress, or Completed.                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Maintenance Template**      | Template de Manutenção         | A predefined model (inspection, scheduled maintenance, overhaul) that dictates the checklist of tasks and the type of maintenance to be performed. It also defines the periodic intervals (flight hours or calendar days) to trigger scheduled maintenance alerts. |
| **Minimum Threshold**         | Limiar Mínimo                  | The specific inventory level defined for a maintenance part that, when reached or crossed, triggers a low-stock alert for the maintenance supervisor.                                                                                                              |
| **Part Number**               | Identificador de Peça          | A unique alphanumeric string that identifies a specific type of maintenance component in the inventory.                                                                                                                                                            |
| **Registration Number**       | Número de Registo              | A unique alphanumeric string that identifies a specific physical aircraft (similar to a license plate), distinct from its general model.                                                                                                                           |
| **Route Status**              | Estado da Rota                 | The current availability of a flight route, being either Active (available for scheduling) or Inactive.                                                                                                                                                            |
| **Runway**                    | Pista                          | A physical surface at an airport where aircraft take off and land, defined by its name, length, and orientation.                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Scheduled Flight**          | Voo Agendado                   | A planned flight resulting from assigning a specific aircraft to a route for a specific date and time, managing its own lifecycle status (Scheduled, Delayed, In-Flight, Completed).                                                                               |
